counter_fiduciary-duty-requires-profit-maximization
"Fiduciary duty requires profit maximization"
Modern fiduciary duty requires considering all material factors that affect risk and return, including ESG factors. The duty is to beneficiaries' best interests, not narrow profit maximization.
Citations: src_ceres-fiduciary-duty, src_pensions-and-investments-esg-fiduciary
COUNTER
counter_esg-investing-reduces-returns
"ESG investing reduces returns"
Academic meta-analyses show neutral to positive performance impact from ESG integration. Many exclusionary screens have minimal impact on diversification when implemented professionally.
Citations: src_morgan-stanley-sustainable-reality, src_nber-esg-performance
COUNTER
counter_exclusions-reduce-diversification
"Exclusions reduce diversification"
Targeted exclusions of specific conduct (not entire sectors) maintain diversification. Factor-neutral rebalancing preserves exposure to size, value, growth, and quality factors.
COUNTER
counter_divestment-has-no-impact
"Divestment has no impact"
Divestment creates reputational pressure and raises capital costs for excluded companies. Combined with engagement, it signals market expectations for better corporate behavior.
